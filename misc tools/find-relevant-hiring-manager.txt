7 steps to find Program Manager roles in the first 24 hours (and know who's hiring)
1/ Search for "[Program Manager] + Hiring" on Linkedin
2/ Click the Posts filter
3/ Go to All Filters > Author Company
4/ Add all of your target companies (target 10)
5/ Select the Date Posted filter + click Past 24 Hours
6/ Bookmark this page for the future
7/ Rinse and repeat daily or weekly
This list provides the latest "hiring posts" in your niche/industry.
The hiring manager = the person you should reach out to
And you just bypassed hundreds of other candidates.
